Anbuthambi Bhojarajan

President of ICT Academy, India

Dr. Anbuthambi is the President of ICT Academy. Has been with ICT Academy since its inception holding various positions and is currently in the leadership role of the organization.

Responsible for leading the company and providing strategic directions towards achieving performance in all its initiatives. Anbu is focused on making ICT Academy a globally credible organization with its industry academia and Government collaboration as an objective. With solid acumen in the domain, Anbu contributes in forging and managing newer partnerships and projects. His strong execution capabilities coupled with strategic planning helps him drive innovation and effective execution across the organization.

With around 20 years of experience in the IT, ITES and Education Industries, Anbu holds a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Application; a Master’s in Business Administration; and a Post-Graduation in Business Management from XLRI, Jamshedpur, with a merit of third rank holder in his batch. He is a member of Governing Councils and Board of Studies in various Higher Educational Institutions across India.
